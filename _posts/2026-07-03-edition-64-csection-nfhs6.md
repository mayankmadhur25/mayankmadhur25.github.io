NFHS-6 puts India's C-section rate at 27.2%, but the real story is the split underneath it: 54.1% in the private sector versus 16.9% in public. The surgical-birth surge is a payment-design problem, not a clinical one. Includes an interactive state-by-state C-Section Tracker built on NFHS-6 data.
